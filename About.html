HISTORICAL BACKGROUND

The Buildings were inherited from "karimjee Jevanjee Foundation" in which they were built since the Year 1947. When it reached 20/2/1991 the Tanga City Council took grant and Established a Secondary which was registered the same Year (1991) under Registration No: S.427.
It started with 80 students in which 40 were Male and 40 were Female. In the Year 1998 the school upgraded from two streams to four streams. However in the year 2002 each stream sub-divided into Seven Classes.
Nowadays, The School has 28 Classrooms from Form One to Form Four.

LOCATION OF SCHOOL

The School is Located in Tanga City Urban - Bombo Area.

WE TEACH


Our vision is to forge strong, positive connections with students so they can achieve independence, confidence, and gain academic knowledge.
